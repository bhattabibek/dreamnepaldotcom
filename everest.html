Everest

Honey (Moon) Trek in Nepal: Everest Three Passes Trek for the Adventure Loving Couple Everest Base Camp (5364 meters) Everest Three Pass Trek for the Adventure loving honeymoon couple? This may surpasses your imagination. Nepal Kameleon Holidays has brought a breathtaking honeymoon package in Nepal for the adventure lovers. Are you a newly married couple? Why don't you make your honeymoon a lifetime experience with the blessings of the highest peak of the world-Mt. Everest (8848 meters).

  Everest Three Passes Trek embarks from Lukla, the Himalayan gateway of Everest. It is about half an hour flight from Kathmandu. Managing your backpack, the journey through Everest three high passes, Gokyo valley and Everest base camp leads you along the trail dominated by the Sherpa communities, who are the best known Himalayan leaders in the world.

   Explore Namche Bazaar, Khumjung monastery, Khunde and the surroundings
You will have options where to move first regarding the route: clockwise and anti-clockwise route. We recommend the clockwise route, which means begin the trek via Thame, Lungden and cross the Renjo La Pass (5340 meters). It leads you to the Gokyo Lake and valley.   An exploration hike to the Gokyo Ri is amazing and highly recommended. The Gokyo Ri is an exceptional viewpoint of the Everest Himalayan chain, including Mt. Mt. Everest, Lhotse, Cho Oyu, Makalu and other peaks. The Everest glacial Lakes of the Khumbu are incredible.

 Heading from Gokyo valley, cross over the Cho La Pass (5330 meters), which is technically challenging. The route includes blue icy trail and a rock falling zone. Descending to Lobuche, you will climb to the Everest base camp (5364 meters) and Kalapatthar (5545 meters).

here lies another challenging section, which is the highest pass among three, known as Khongma La Pass (5555 meters). A long ascending and descending section makes the trek technically challenging. Descending to Chhukung, join the main trail of Everest Base Camp (EBC) Via Dingbuche, trek down continually through Tengbuche, Namche Bazaar, Phakding and Lukla, where the trek concludes and fly back to Kathmandu.

    Note: You need to manage some extra days, which will be useful in case of flight cancellation, which is common in the Everest sector.
 

Facts

Total Duration: 20 Days
Highest Altitude: 5555 meters (Khongma La Pass)
Transportation: Flight (opt: helicopter)
Sector: Everest (Khumbu)
High Passes: Renjo La Pass (5340 meters), Cho La Pass (5330 meters), Khongma La Pass (5555 meters)
 
Highlights Namche Bazaar, Thame, Renjo La Pass, Gokyo Valley, Gokyo Ri, Cho La Pass, EBC, Kalapatthar, Khongma La pass, Chhukung, Dingbuche and Tengbuche Monastery

 Itinerary Day 01: Arrival at Kathmandu (1350 meters) and transfer to hotel.
Day 02: Kathmandu sightseeing and trek preparation.
Day 03: Fly from Kathmandu to Lukla (2800 meters), walk to Phakding (2640 meters).
Day 04: Walk from Phakding to Namche Bazaar (3440 meters).
Day 05: Exploration and acclimatization day.
Day 06: Walk to Thame (3820m).
Day 07: Walk to Lumde (4368m).
Day 08: Walk to Gokyo (4790m) via Renjo La Pass (5340m).
Day 09: Explore around Gokyo valley, hike to Gokyo Ri (5360 meters).
Day 10: Walk from Gokyo Lake to Thangnag (4765 meters).
Day 11: Walk from Thangnag to Dzongla (4830m) via Cho La Pass (5330 meters).
 Day 12: Walk from Dzongla to Lobuche (4910 meters).
Day 13: Walk from Lobuche to Gorakshep (5170 meters)-hike to Everest base camp (5364 meters) and descend to Gorakshep.
Day 14: Walk to Kalapatthar (5550 meters), climb down to Gorakshep and to Lobuche (4280 meters).
Day 15: Walk from Lobuche to Chhukung via Khongma La Pass (5555 meters).
Day 16: Walk from Chhukung to Kyangjuma.
Day 17: Walk from Kyangjuma to Monju (2835 meters).
Day 18: Walk from Monju to Lukla.
Day 19: Fly back from Lukla to Kathmandu.
Day 20: Departure.
