Mountain Biking

- 2+ days
- Best season: Autumn, winter, spring

Ride with the pioneers of Himalayan Mountain Biking. The land of mountains has everything a biker could wish for: breath-taking Himalayan landscapes, centuries-old cultures, fresh mountain air —and truly abysmal roads.
Most of the best mountain biking roads are in the Kathmandu Valley because, since ironically, the real mountains are just too high and too steep.
Riding in and around the Kathmandu Valley takes you into a world of traditional Newari Villages and breath-taking scenery. Many of the villages in the area have got lost in time and they are still built the old and traditional way.
Mountain biking in Nepal is always a colourful cultural experience – and this is a major attraction for most bikers.

Our Mountain Biking Tour itineraries are customized for every group individually.
